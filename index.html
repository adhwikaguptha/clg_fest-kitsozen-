About

"At Kamala Institute of Technology and Science, we proudly present Kitsozen'25, our premier annual techno-cultural festival. Established in 2007, KITS has been committed to nurturing talent and fostering innovation. Kitsozen’25 not only nurtures cutting-edge research and skill development but also fortifies interdisciplinary collaboration and industry engagement by bringing together bright minds from diverse fields.

Through a dynamic blend of technical competitions, hands-on workshops, cultural performances, and entrepreneurial initiatives, the event fosters an environment where innovation thrives. It serves as a platform for students to apply their knowledge in real-world scenarios, engage with industry experts, and explore emerging technologies. By encouraging teamwork, creativity, and a spirit of exploration, Kitsozen’25 plays a pivotal role in shaping future leaders and innovators, preparing them to excel in an ever-evolving global landscape.
